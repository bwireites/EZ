Frequently Asked Questions

What documents do I need to sell gold or get a loan?

You will need one valid government-issued photo ID (like an Aadhar Card, Passport, or Driver's License) and one address proof. For loans, a PAN card may also be required.

How is the purity of my gold determined?

We use non-invasive, high-precision XRF (X-ray fluorescence) spectrometers to accurately determine the purity of your gold without causing any damage to your items.
